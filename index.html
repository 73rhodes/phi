De Divina Proportione

Van de Graaf's canon for single-column broadsheet. Incorporating the back margins into the text area means the text area aren't in proportion to the page, but the left foredge, right foredge, top and foot margins end up being the same as verso/recto book layout.

Van de Graaf's canon works on pages of any proportion, which is well-suited to scrolling media like the web.
